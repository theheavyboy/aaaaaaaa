Appeal Guide

Before You Submit: Gather all relevant documentation and evidence that supports your appeal. This includes account history, communications, and any proof that you violated our policies unintentionally.

What to Include: Provide a detailed, honest explanation of your situation. Avoid making excuses. Instead, explain what happened, why it happened, and what steps you've taken to prevent it in the future. Be concise but thorough.

After Submission: Our review team will examine your appeal carefully. We will contact you within 24-48 hours with a decision. In some cases, particularly complex appeals may take up to 7 business days.

Possible Outcomes: Your page may be reinstated immediately, your page may remain inactive pending further investigation, or your page may be permanently removed if we find the violation was intentional or severe.

💡 Tip: Appeals are more successful when you take responsibility for your actions and demonstrate a clear understanding of the policy you violated.
